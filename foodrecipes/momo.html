Momo's Recipe

Description

Momo is a traditional Nepalese dish consisting of steamed dumplings filled with a savory mixture of minced meat, vegetables, and spices. These dumplings are typically served with a spicy tomato-based dipping sauce and are a favorite snack or appetizer in Nepal and neighboring regions.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Minced meat (chicken, buff or goat)
  • Finely chopped vegetables (onions, cabbage)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, water, and a pinch of salt to make a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by mixing minced meat, finely chopped vegetables, minced garlic, minced ginger, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle and fold the edges to seal, creating a crescent-shaped dumpling.
  5. Steam the momos in a steamer basket for about 10-12 minutes, or until the dough is cooked and the filling is tender.
  6. Serve hot momos with spicy tomato-based dipping sauce.
