Spaghetti's Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made from long, thin noodles typically served with a sauce. The noodles are usually boiled until they are tender but still slightly firm to the bite, then topped with a variety of sauces such as marinara, bolognese, or Alfredo. Spaghetti is a versatile and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed with simple ingredients or dressed up with additional toppings like meatballs or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Grated cheese (Parmesan or mozzarella)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti noodles to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente, usually about 8-10 minutes.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the sauce. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© minced garlic and chopped onion until softened and fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the pan and season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Once the spaghetti noodles are cooked, drain them well and transfer them to a serving dish.
  6. Pour the tomato sauce over the cooked spaghetti and toss gently to coat the noodles evenly.
  7. Serve hot spaghetti topped with grated cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
